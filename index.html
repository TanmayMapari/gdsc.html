 Goals of the gdsc program
	
	The Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) program aims to empower university students with practical 
skills in areas like Android development, cloud computing, AI, and more. It provides a platform 
for students to connect, learn, and collaborate on projects, workshops, and events that enhance 
their technical and professional growth. The GDSC program aims to equip university students with
 practical skills in various tech domains and foster a community for learning,
 collaboration, and project development.
